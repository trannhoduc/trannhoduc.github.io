About Me

Researcher, Engineer & Educator

I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at Mid Sweden University, working on robust transmission strategies for remote state estimation in networked control systems. My expected thesis tackles making remote decisions reliably — even when communication channels are noisy or under attack.

Before doctoral studies, I worked across industry and academia in Vietnam — as an Admissions Specialist at HCMUT, a Product Engineer at NSJ Group, and a Firmware Engineer at Homa Tech. This mix of experience grounds my research in real-world constraints.

I care about clear writing, reproducible research, and teaching that actually sticks. In 2025 I taught Probability and Random Processes at Mid Sweden University — a course I find both beautiful and deeply practical.
