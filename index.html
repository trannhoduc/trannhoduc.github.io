About Me

A bit about who I am

Hi — I'm Alex, a software engineer based in San Francisco. I've spent the past 6 years building web applications, distributed backends, and the internal tools that help teams move faster.

I care deeply about clean abstractions, developer experience, and systems that don't page you at 3 AM. Before writing code full-time, I studied Computer Science at UC Berkeley and got mildly obsessed with compilers.

When I'm not in front of a screen, I'm likely hiking in the Sierras, reading sci-fi, or failing to learn Mandarin.
